<h2> Overview</h2>
<p>
<b>Research Paper on Clinical Trials</b> is an academic project that explains how clinical trials are conducted,
their importance in healthcare innovation, and how researchers evaluate new treatments and medical technologies.
The repository contains structured research material that helps students, researchers, and healthcare enthusiasts
understand the <b>process, design, and impact of clinical trials</b>.
</p>

<blockquote>
A research-driven project focused on understanding how clinical trials contribute to safe and effective medical advancements.
</blockquote>

<h2> Key Features</h2>
<ul>
    <li>📑 <b>Comprehensive Research</b> — Covers the fundamentals of clinical trials and their significance.</li>
    <li>🧪 <b>Clinical Trial Phases Explained</b> — Detailed explanation of Phase I, II, III, and IV trials.</li>
    <li>📊 <b>Scientific Analysis</b> — Discussion on methodology, evaluation, and results interpretation.</li>
